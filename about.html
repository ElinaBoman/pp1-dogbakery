Here our favorite guests are dogs! And our main goal is to spoil dogs with treats, play and tons of love! We have a wide selection of treats, produced in our own bakery! All of our products are fresh and without preservatives.

How it started...

My name is Elina and I'm the founder of Dog Barkery. I'm also the very happy owner of my amazing dog Lou! You can see her here lying in the grass.
A few years ago Lou got really sick from being allergic to storebought treats and foods. That's when the dream of a dog bakery started growing. A place where all the dog treats would be fresh and healthy for dogs. A few years later the Dog Barkery opened its door for the first time and a few years after that we are happy to present our additional activity center. We welcome owners and dogs of all sizes, breeds and ages. It's our opinion that all dogs needs a real good spoiling from time to time. We look forward to be able to spoil your dog with a really good time and create really happy memories for you both!
