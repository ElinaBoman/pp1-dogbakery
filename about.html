Welcome to Dog Barkery!

Bakery and activity center for dogs!


Here our favorite guests are dogs! And our main goal is to spoil dogs with treats, play and toons of love! We have a wide selection of treats, produced in our own bakery! All of our products are fresh and without preservatives.


During the week we organize different activities for you and your dog. How about nosework? Or maybe try our agility course? Check out our weekly schedule bellow and follow our social medias where we keep you up to date with all of our awsome activities!


Psst!

While our main focus is on our four legged friends we serve drinks and homebaked goods for our two legged friends aswell.
